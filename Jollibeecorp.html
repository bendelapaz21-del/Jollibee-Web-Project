About Us

Jollibee Foods Corporation (JFC) is a Filipino multinational fast food company founded in 1975. Today, it operates over 6,600 stores across 34 countries, serving customers through beloved brands like Jollibee, Chowking, Greenwich, and The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf.
